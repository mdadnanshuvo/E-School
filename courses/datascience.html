Data Science

In a Data Science course, students will learn how to use various tools and techniques to manipulate and analyze data, extract valuable insights, and make data-driven decisions. They will also learn how to apply machine learning algorithms to solve real-world problems and gain hands-on experience working with large datasets.

  • Collect, organize, and store data from various sources.
  • Clean, prepare, and transform data for analysis.
  • Analyze and visualize data using statistical and graphical techniques.
  • Apply machine learning algorithms to solve real-world problems.
  • Communicate findings and recommendations based on data analysis.
  • Understand and use various tools and technologies commonly used in Data Science, such as programming languages (e.g. Python, R) and data analysis libraries (e.g. NumPy, Pandas).

Tentative time to complete this course:

  • Prior knowledge and experience: Students who have a strong foundation in computer science concepts and programming may be able to complete the course in less time than those who are new to these topics.
  • Pace of the course: Some Data Science courses may be taught at a faster pace than others, which could affect the amount of time it takes to complete the course.
